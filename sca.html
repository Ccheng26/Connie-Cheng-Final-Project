Supply Chain Analyst

The Supply Chain Analyst will manage supply chain functions for all EVAid products. The ideal candidate will analyze a variety of information sources including the customersâ€™ systems, customer specific data, and syndicated third party data to identify supply chain opportunities and resolutions. Will be required to interface directly with the account to manage and maintain desired supply goals and objectives.

The ideal candidate will possess excellent communication, organizational, multi-tasking and problem-solving skills. An aptitude to function within deadlines while working both independently and as a part of a team is essential.

All employees must be able to pass our background check, drug-screen, and MVR driving record check.

Tasks and Responsibilities

  • Gathers data and conducts analysis with the goal of improving the organization's supply chain operations.
  • Perform daily, weekly and monthly reviews and analyses of current processes using operational metrics and reports.
  • Identifies underperforming areas in the supply chain (including, but not limited to" operations, purchasing, inventory, and distribution) and suggests improvements or resolutions to problems.
  • Actively engage and work closely with colleagues to understand how EVAid operates and where improvements can be made to support the company's business model.
  • Understand and communicate the financial and operational impact of any changes.
  • Investigate and provide resolution to any issues the customer encounters.
  • Suggest changes to senior management using analytics to support your recommendations. Actively participate in the implementation of approved changes.
  • Create informative, actionable and repeatable reporting that highlights relevant business trends and opportunities for improvement.
  • Conduct insightful, ad hoc analyses to investigate ongoing or one-time operational issues.

Requirements

The qualified candidate must have a Bachelor's degree, in addtion to 2 to 5 years experience working at a manufacturing or distribution company. A firm understanding of purchasing, inventory and warehouse operational processes. The ability to impact operations and effect change without being confrontational which demands excellent verbal and written communication skills. The Supply Chain Analyst must be betail oriented, analytical and inquisitive.Have the ability to work independently or with others and be extremely organized with strong time-management skills.

Microsoft Access and/or SQL experience strongly preferred.
